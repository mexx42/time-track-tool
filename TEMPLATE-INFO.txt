Name: time-todo-abo
Description: Modular tracker for Time-Tracking, absence/vacation
 information for the users and other features. It also implements a
 newspaper subscription tracker with customer database, tracking of
 invoices, etc. Features can individually be
 turned on and off by customizing the includes in schema.py
Intended-for: Time-Tracking, IT-Issue tracking, Subscription tracking
 and others.
